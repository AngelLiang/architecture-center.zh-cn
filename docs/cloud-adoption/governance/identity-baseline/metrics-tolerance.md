## <a name="risk-tolerance-indicators"></a><span data-ttu-id="e1120-134">风险容忍度指示器</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-134">Risk tolerance indicators</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e1120-135">与标识基线相关的风险在很大程度上与组织的标识基础结构复杂性相关。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-135">Risks related to Identity Baseline are largely related to the complexity of your organization's identity infrastructure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-136">如果所有用户和组都使用单个目录或与其他服务进行最低程度集成的云原生标识提供者进行管理，则风险级别可能较小。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-136">If all of your users and groups are managed using a single directory or cloud native identity provider using minimal integration with other services, your risk level will likely be small.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-137">但是，随着业务需求增长，标识基线系统可能需要支持更复杂的方案（如多个目录），以支持内部组织或与外部标识提供者的联合。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-137">However, as your business needs grow your Identity Baseline systems may need to support more complicated scenarios, such as multiple directories to support your internal organization or federation with external identity providers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-138">随着这些系统变得更加复杂，风险会增加。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-138">As these systems become more complex, risk increases.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e1120-139">在云采用的早期阶段，与 IT 安全团队和业务利益干系人合作确定与标识相关的[业务风险](business-risks.md)，然后确定标识风险容忍度的可接受基线。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-139">In the early stages of cloud adoption, work with your IT security team and business stakeholders to identify [business risks](business-risks.md) related to identity, then determine an acceptable baseline for identity risk tolerance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-140">CAF 指导的这一部分提供了一些示例，但公司或部署的详细风险和基线可能有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-140">This section of the CAF guidance provides examples, but the detailed risks and baselines for your company or deployments may be different.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e1120-141">有了基线后，就可以建立表示所确定的不可接受的风险增加的最小基准。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-141">Once you have a baseline, establish minimum benchmarks representing an unacceptable increase in your identified risks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-142">当你需要采取措施来缓解这些风险时，这些基准将作为触发器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-142">These benchmarks act as triggers for when you need to take action to mitigate these risks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-143">下面是一些有关标识相关指标（如上文所述）如何证明在标识基线规则中增加投资的合理性的示例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-143">The following are a few examples of how identity related metrics, such as those discussed above, can justify an increased investment in the Identity Baseline discipline.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-144">**用户帐户数触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-144">**User account number trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-145">在标识系统中管理的用户、组或其他对象数超过 X 的公司可以从标识基线规则投资中受益，以确保对大量帐户进行高效治理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-145">A company with more than X number of users, groups, or other objects managed in your identity systems could benefit from investment in the Identity Baseline discipline to ensure efficient governance over a large number of accounts.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-146">**本地标识依赖项触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-146">**On-premises identity dependency trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-147">计划将工作负荷迁移到需要旧式身份验证功能或第三方 MFA 的云的公司应投资标识基线规则，以减少与重构或其他云基础结构部署相关的风险。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-147">A company planning to migrate workloads to the cloud that require legacy authentication capabilities or third party MFA should invest in the Identity Baseline discipline to reduce risks related to refactoring or additional cloud infrastructure deployment.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-148">**目录服务复杂性触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-148">**Directory services complexity trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-149">维护的单独林、域或目录租户数超过 X 的公司应投资标识基线规则，以减少与帐户管理相关的风险以及与跨多个系统分散的多个用户凭据相关的效率问题。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-149">A company maintaining more than X number of individual forests, domains, or directory tenants should invest in the Identity Baseline discipline to reduce risks related with account management and the efficiency issues related to multiple user credentials spread across multiple systems.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-150">**云托管目录服务触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-150">**Cloud hosted directory services trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-151">在云中托管 X 个 Active Directory 服务器虚拟机 (VM)，或这些基于云的服务器上管理 X 个组织单位的公司可以从标识基线规则投资中受益，以优化与任何本地或其他外部标识服务的集成。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-151">A company hosting X number of Active Directory server virtual machines (VMs) hosted in the cloud, or having X number of Organizational Units managed on these cloud-based servers, can benefit from investment in the Identity Baseline discipline to optimize integration with any on-premises or other external identity services.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-152">**联合触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-152">**Federation trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-153">与 X 个外部标识基线系统进行联合身份验证的公司可以从标识基线规则投资中受益，以确保在联合成员间实施一致的组织策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-153">A company implementing identity federation with X number of external Identity Baseline systems can benefit from investing in the Identity Baseline discipline to ensure consistent organizational policy across federation members.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-154">**提升的访问权限触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-154">**Elevated access trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-155">有超过 X% 的用户对管理工具和资源拥有提升的权限的公司应考虑投资标识基线规则，以尽量减少无意中过度预配用户访问权限的风险。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-155">A company with more than X% of users with elevated permissions to management tools and resources should consider investing in the Identity Baseline discipline to minimize the risk of inadvertent over-provisioning of access to users.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-156">**RBAC 触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-156">**RBAC trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-157">使用基于角色的访问控制方法的资源低于 X% 的公司应考虑投资标识基线规则，以确定向用户分配资源访问权限的优化方法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-157">A company with under X% of resources using role-based access control methods should consider investing in the Identity Baseline discipline to identify optimized ways to assign user access to resources.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-158">**身份验证失败触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-158">**Authentication failure trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-159">身份验证失败尝试次数超过 X% 的公司应投资标识基线规则，以确保身份验证方法不受到外部攻击，并且用户能够正确使用身份验证方法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-159">A company where authentication failures represent more than X% of attempts should invest in the Identity Baseline discipline to ensure that authentication methods are not under external attack, and that users are able to use the authentication methods correctly.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e1120-160">**授权失败触发器**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-160">**Authorization failure trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1120-161">被拒绝的访问尝试次数超过 X% 的公司应投资标识基线规则，以改进访问控制的应用和更新，并确定潜在的恶意访问尝试。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1120-161">A company where access attempts are rejected more than X% of the time should invest in the Identity Baseline discipline to improve the application and updating of access controls, and identify potentially malicious access attempts.</span></span>
